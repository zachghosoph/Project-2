Finding the Right Company

There are many companies to buy from when it comes to humane food. This can make it difficult to sort humane companies from ones that just have similar standards.

Labels to Look For:

Certified Organic

Certified Humane

Certified Animal Welfare Improved

What the Labels Mean

Certified Organic

The certified organic label indicates that meat has been fed livestock agricultural feed products which are 100% organic. It also states that grazing animals must be on a pasture for at least 120 days per year. All other organic livestock are required to have outdoor access year round.

Certified Humane/Animal Welfare Improved

These standards indicate that a company follows guidelines of a non-profit organization such as A Greener World, or Humane Farm Animal Care. These organizations both award these certifications, and run yearly checks to verify them.
