How to Help

Individual Help

The issue of livestock welfare may seem out of an individuals control. However there are many ways an individual can make a positive impact on the issue. These include small changes, from purchasing habits to helping to change the habits of others around you.

Factory Farming

In order to advocate for better welfare standards, the most logical thing to do is purchase from producers who support better welfare standards. Many companies have standards that can be researched.

Humane Standard:

One standard quite common among these companies is the Certified Humane Standard. These companies guarantee that their livestock is able to perform natural processes. They also guarantee that their animals are reared free from cages, and allotted space to move around. Animals are also fed higher quality diets than their confined counterparts.

How You Can Help:

Buying from companies that advocate for animal welfare

Buying from Companies with Certified Humane Standard

Becoming informed about company production standards
